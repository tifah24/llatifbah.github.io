ABOUT ME

Greetings ! My name is Latifah Bah. I am currently on my own journey to figuring out what works best for me and my mental health. Join me and lets find ways to help keep us afloat !

Three years ago, I experienced the most traumatizing event in my life. I was lost and even still at times, I feel this way. During my journey I have become very enthralled with the well being of not only my mental but others as well. I have found ways to keep me grounded and at peace when my mind seems like nothing but chaos. I want to share and be able to help someone who may be in my position before learning ways to help myself.                     

Here is a space for resources! I will share different ways that help me when I feel my mental is not at its best. Along with other resources including, contacts and websites. Hopefully some or even one at the least will help you on your journey.

Life doesn't stop for anyone. Always remember to KEEP GOING!
